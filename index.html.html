OUR THESIS

Energy access changes what a school can teach.

No power means no labs, no fans in summer, no lights for evening classes, no working computers. Energy access isn't a side issue for under-resourced schools — it's the bottleneck underneath everything else.

WHY SCHOOLS

Schools are the highest-leverage place to deploy solar.

A solarized school doesn't just save money — it becomes a living classroom for climate and energy literacy, and a visible, trusted proof point for the whole community around it.

WHERE WE ARE

Small, founder-led, and building deliberately.

OPOH is a young, student-run initiative. We're not a large NGO with a big back office — we're a focused team proving a model with Lumina, and now scaling it through Belaku. We'd rather be honest about our stage than overstate it.

Early and serious — not hidden, not inflated
THE MODEL

Prove it once. Replicate it everywhere.

Lumina proved school solarization works. Belaku takes the lesson further — solar light plus energy education, designed from day one to scale across government schools.
